About Us

Welcome to Barangay Lumingon, where community, culture, and progress come together! As a vibrant and diverse neighborhood, we are dedicated to fostering a safe, inclusive, and supportive environment for all our residents. Our barangay is committed to promoting local initiatives that enhance the well-being of our community, from health services and education programs to environmental sustainability and livelihood projects. Guided by strong leadership and the spirit of bayanihan, we work together to address the needs of our citizens and create opportunities for growth. Join us in building a brighter future for Barangay Lumingon!
